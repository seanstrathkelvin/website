Flowcell

FC100

Cell for continuous in-line oxygen monitoring or for connection to a flow-through respirometer chamber.

Features

  • Uniquely designed electrode holder immerses electrode tip into sample flow
  • Easily disassembled for cleaning
  • Excellent visibility of flowline
  • Easily mountable to the level of the flowline or for immersion

Overview

The acrylic chamber of the flowcell forms a threaded connection to the integral electrode holder. Only the tip of the electrode projects into the water flow. The inlet and outlet tubes are made from stainless steel. To enable the flowcell to be immersed below the surface of the constant temperature bath containing the respirometer chamber, the electrode holder is fitted with a collar for attachment to a vertical or horizontal 6.25 mm diameter laboratory rod.
