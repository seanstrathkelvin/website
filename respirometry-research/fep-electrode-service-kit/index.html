FEP Electrode Service Kit

SI021

Service kit for our oxygen electrodes containing FEP membranes.

Contents

  • 12 × FEP Membranes
  • 12 × Abrasives
  • 35 ml of Electrolyte Solution
  • 5 × O-Rings
  • 1 × O-Ring Applicator
  • 1 × FEP Jacket

Overview

The SI021 FEP Service Kit is used to service our range of respirometry research products for mitochondria experimentation. The service kit contains 12 fluorinated ethylene propylene (FEP) membranes. For other experimentation, please see our SI020 PP Electrode Service Kit.
