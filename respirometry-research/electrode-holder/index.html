Electrode Holder

EH100

Electrode holder for use with custom-designed respiration chambers.

Specification

Electrode Holder:

  • Length: 85 mm
  • Diameter: 13 mm
  • Material: Black Acetal

Acrylic Collar:

  • Length: 10 mm
  • Diameter: 26 mm
  • Material: Black Acetal

Overview

The electrode holder is a black acetal cylinder in which the electrode is located so that its tip projects from the end. The holder is used for the insertion of the electrode into the customers’ own respiration chamber. An acrylic collar containing an O-ring on its inside surface is also provided. This can be cemented over a 13 mm hole drilled in the wall of the respiration chamber to facilitate insertion of the electrode holder. The electrode should never be used other than in an electrode holder, to avoid ground-leak damage to the electrode.
