Dual Channel Oxygen Meter

SI782

Oxygen meter that operates with one or two microcathode oxygen electrodes for precision dissolved oxygen measurement.

Features

  • May be used with the complete range of oxygen measurement cells and chambers from Strathkelvin Instruments
  • Range of dissolved oxygen and respiration units selectable
  • Sophisticated software enables automatic calculation and reporting of respiration rates

Overview

Strathkelvin's SI782 Dual Channel Oxygen Meter is a new concept in precision dissolved oxygen measurement. It can either act as a stand-alone oxygen meter or be connected to our respirometry and monitoring software on your own PC, which is supplied with all our respirometry research products.

Either one or two of our SI1302 Microcathode Electrodes can be connected to the meter. The digital display can be set to read in units of PO2 (torr, mm Hg, kPa) or concentration (% saturation, ppm, mg/l, or µmol/l).

The software is virtually identical to the software for the SI929 Six-Channel Oxygen Meter, with the only difference being that you will see only one or two traces displayed instead of six. A comprehensive, easy-to-follow instruction manual is supplied, which will be particularly valuable to anyone who is not familiar with respirometry work.
