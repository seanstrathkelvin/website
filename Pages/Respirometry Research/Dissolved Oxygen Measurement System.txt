--Overview--
Our precision dissolved oxygen measurement systems and accessories are successfully operating in teaching and research laboratories worldwide.
 
Combined with Strathkelvin's respirometry software, our dissolved oxygen meters and respiration chambers are providing accurate and rapid measurement of dissolved oxygen and respiration rates across a wide number of life science fields. 

The following three parts are required for a complete dissolved oxygen measurement system:
# Microcathode Oxygen Electrode
# Oxygen Meter
# Respiration Chamber or Cell

@ProductSection(Microcathode Oxygen Electrode)

--Oxygen Meters--
An oxygen meter is required to measure the current generated by the microcathode oxygen electrode. Strathkelvin has two different oxygen meters that can be used as part of a dissolved oxygen measurement system.

The @ProductLink(Dual Channel Oxygen Meter)[SI782 Dual Channel Oxygen Meter] is an oxygen meter that can operate with one or two oxygen electrodes.

The @ProductLink(Six-Channel Oxygen Meter)[SI928 Six-Channel Oxygen Meter] is an oxygen meter for precision dissolved oxygen measurement that can operate with up to six microcathode oxygen electrodes simultaneously.

--Respiration Chambers and Cells--
Strathkelvin offers a wide range of measurement chambers and cells for respiration for a variety of different purposes. All our chambers and cells are designed to work with our microcathode oxygen electrodes and any of our oxygen meters.
